About Daily Vim

Hey! I'm Thomas, the creator of Daily Vim.

Learning Vim can be a daunting task, but that is where Daily Vim comes in to break it down into bite-sized chunks.

Each day, you'll find a new Vim tip or trick that I've discovered and found useful in my own coding. Some days it will be a simple command, other days a productivity hack. But it will always be something small, you can immediately apply to your own Vim usage.

If you want to contribute your own tricks and ideas, feel free to open a pull-request with your changes in the data/tips.json file.

Happy Vimming!
